EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of January 1, 2025, between TechCorp Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of Hungary ("Company"), and John Smith, an individual ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Senior Software Developer and shall perform such duties as may be assigned by the Company's management team.

2. COMPENSATION
The Company shall pay Employee an annual salary of 8,000,000 HUF, payable in monthly installments of 666,667 HUF.

3. WORKING HOURS
Employee's regular working hours shall be 40 hours per week, Monday through Friday.

4. PROBATIONARY PERIOD
The first 6 months of employment shall constitute a probationary period during which either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information of the Company.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination, Employee shall return all Company property.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Hungary.
